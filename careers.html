FAQs

How can I reset my progress?

Go to your home page / gallery. Scroll to the very bottom and locate the reset button. Note, if you choose to reset your progress you will not be able to recover it in the future

How can I buy more pictures for my gallery?

Play games! Navigate to the games page to play. Playing games earning you lots of coins which you can use to buy stickers. Tip: playing on challenge mode gives you the opputunity to earn extra coins.

I've bought all the pictures in the shop, now what?

Don't worry! New stickers are coming soon

Who is David Attenborough?

Sir David Attenborough is an English broadcaster and biologist. He is best known for writing and presenting the nine natural history documentary series forming the Life collection, a comprehensive survey of animal and plant life on Earth.
